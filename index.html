The first unit
Wiibike Vietnam is proud to be the FIRST unit to manufacture electric power-assisted bicycles in Vietnam. Although newly established for nearly 2 years, Wiibike carries within itself the spirit of a young, modern and constantly innovating startup; improve products as well as service quality.

Provide urban transport solutions 4.0
With the mission of providing urban transport solutions 4.0, Wiibike focuses on developing product lines using clean, environmentally friendly energy. In particular, applying the Lithium battery line that won the Nobel Prize in Chemistry 2019, Wiibike products stand out with the following characteristics: Light - Compact - Safe.

cooperation with Thong Nhat bicycle brand
Along with the self-research process, Wiibike also cooperates with Thong Nhat bicycle brand to complete the design, production process and launch many product lines. In 2019, Wiibike officially launched commercial activities of electric power-assisted bicycle products and received a lot of support from customers.
